Cortana

Better Integration

Dedicated Volume Control

The audio mixer on Windows Phone 8.1 is broken down into two sections: Ringer + Notifications and Media + Apps. Cortana falls into the Media + Apps category because she is an app. But she is more than just an app, she is integrated with the OS. Therefore she shouldn't play by the same restrictions that the volume of Media + Apps do. Giving Cortana her own volume control in the audio mixer will further complete her integration with the OS. If I don't want to hear Cortana, I don't ask a question via voice.

Reminders

I use location reminders all the time. Sometimes I can't always complete the task right away, but I'm only given two options: Next Time and Complete. There's needs to be a snooze option with different timers just like the Calendar App.

Calendar Alert

Use Cortana's visual style with calendar alerts, just like reminders have.
